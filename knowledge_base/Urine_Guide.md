Urine Routine Examination (Urinalysis) Knowledge Base
Overview

Urinalysis is one of the most commonly performed laboratory tests. It evaluates:

Kidney function
Urinary tract health
Hydration status
Metabolic disorders
Infections
Liver disorders
Diabetes-related abnormalities

A complete urine examination generally consists of:

Physical Examination
Chemical Examination
Microscopic Examination
